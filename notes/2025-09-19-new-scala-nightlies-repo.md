The approach to publishing Scala nightlies has changed. Nightly builds are now published to a dedicated repository, separate from the main Scala releases. Don't forget to update your build configurations accordingly.

- [A new repository for Scala nightlies | The Scala Programming Language](https://www.scala-lang.org/news/new-scala-nightlies-repo.html)
